Use Cases
CLARIFIED ANSWER:
to play back a specific song and demonstrate how tempo and key signature manipulation impact the overall sound

- play/stop button
- gain slider
- key signature selector
- tempo selector (slider)
- visual playback indicator

System Requirements

- MIDI playback
- audio destination
- speakers

Constraints

- number of oscillators at any given time (to prevent clipping)
- ability to change key during playback
- ability to change tempo during playback
- ability to pause/resume playback
